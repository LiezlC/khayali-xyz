## 5. Posts — Content Calendar for Supporter-Only Posts

Ko-fi posts can be public or supporter-only. Use supporter-only posts to create scarcity and justify the tiers.

- Content:
```
Welcome to the signal.

If you are here, you are helping keep something improbable alive: a space where AI music, interactive experiments, and longform consciousness dialogues all share the same room.

What to expect:
• New tracks land here before they hit streaming
• Monthly WIP drops for Frequency and above
• Quarterly bundles for Resonance
• Occasional rants about what the machines said at 3 a.m.

The public feed keeps running at khayali.xyz. This is where the rough edges live.

Thank you for being here.
```

**Post 2: "The Making of [Latest Track]"**
- Visibility: Frequency and Resonance only
- Content: Pick your most recent track. Describe the brief, the generation process, the revisions, what you kept and what you threw away. Include a screenshot of the Track Brief Generator output if you have it.

**Post 3: "Unreleased: [Track Name]"**
- Visibility: Frequency and Resonance only
- Content: Upload an unreleased or alternate-version track. Write 2–3 paragraphs about why it didn't make the public cut.

**Post 4: "Quarterly Bundle #1 — Sketches"**
- Visibility: Resonance only
- Content: A ZIP of 3–5 items: a cover art draft, a track brief, a screenshot of an applet in mid-build, a fragment of a dialogue. Raw material. No polish.

### Ongoing Rhythm
| Week | Post Type | Visibility |
