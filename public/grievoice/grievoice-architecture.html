Modular Design Philosophy

All intake channels feed into the same processing layer and unified database. New channels can be added without modifying existing infrastructure. Categories, languages, and urgency rules are configurable per deployment. The system is designed for contexts where workers have varying levels of literacy, technology access, and comfort with different communication methods.
