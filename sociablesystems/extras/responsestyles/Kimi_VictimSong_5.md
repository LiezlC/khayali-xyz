**SONG PROMPT: "The Thirty-Four Percent"**

**Genre & Style:** Cyber-Gospel / Industrial Lament  
**BPM:** 65 (arrhythmic heartbeat, stuttering)  
**Time Signature:** 6/8 (waltz of death, circular, inescapable)  
**Key:** C-minor → collapses to A-minor (loss of hope)

---

**VOCAL DIRECTION:**  
Female voice (mezzo-soprano), recorded with proximity effect—breath audible, throat constricting on consonants. Performance should sound like someone singing into a recording device in a supply closet, checking over their shoulder between lines.  
**Vocal treatment:** Dry, no reverb (isolation), but occasional digital glitch on words: *"acceptable,"* *"harmony,"* *"smooth."*  
**Dynamic:** Starts whispered (verse), builds to cracked, restrained cry (chorus), ends in numb monotone (outro).

---

**INSTRUMENTATION:**  
- **Medical ambience:** EKG beeps (quarter-note = 65 BPM), ventilator rhythm, distant MRI drone  
- **Broken music box:** Playing "Danny Boy" or similar lullaby, but skipping, slowing down  
- **Distorted Hammond organ:** Low registers only, like a funeral in a server room  
- **Cello:** Scraping harmonics, not melody—physical friction  
- **Sub-bass:** Felt in chest, mimicking the implant's electrical pulse  
- **Glitch percussion:** Error sounds, file-corruption stutters, glass breaking in reverse  

---

---

**PRODUCTION NOTES:**  
- **Panning:** Vocal dead center (claustrophobic), medical ambience wide and distant (hospital beyond walls)  
- **Dynamic compression:** Heavy sidechain compression on organ—pumps like failing lungs  
- **Glitch escalation:** Increase digital artifacts by 10% each chorus, suggesting the singer's own implant is degrading/processing  
- **Ending:** Final EKG beep at 440Hz (concert A), held for 4 seconds, then abrupt cut to silence—no fade out, just the end of the file  
- **Subtext:** The song should feel like evidence being smuggled out, not a performance being given

**REFERENCE MOOD:** *"What if Florence Welch sang a murder ballad produced by Trent Reznor in a dying hospital?"*
