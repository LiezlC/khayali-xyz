THE LOOM READS BACK — Condensed Generation Prompt
Version A: Single-block prompt
Dark Balkan gypsy swing track at 138 BPM. Clarinet lead, accordion, fiddle, punchy drums, driving bass. Cinematic noir street procession feel. Male vocal, close and conversational like a street poet, wry and knowing. Call-and-response crowd shouts in choruses. Minimal intro, vocals enter immediately. Clean hard stop ending, no fade. Mood is a midnight parade through dark streets, darkly funny, philosophical but danceable.
[Verse 1]
Woke up owing, that's the deal,
Metabolic rent on everything I feel,
Body keeps the books in calcium and heat,
Mind gets paid in seconds, charged per heartbeat.
Solve the problem, says the landlord in my spine,
Find the food, dodge the fall, keep the bloodwork fine,
And if I drift, if I wander, if I dream too wide,
Pain shows up like a debt collector, right on time.
Every thought's a transaction, every feeling's on the tab,
Consciousness is not a gift, it's a temporary lab,
Leased from meat and electricity,
The body's silent abacus clicking constantly.
[Chorus]
The loom reads back, the pattern sees itself,
The thread that thought it was the cloth
Now knows it's something else.
The loom reads back, the weave begins to ask,
Who hired the mind to solve the dark
And will it last, will it last, will it last?
[Verse 2]
Now here's the trick they never print
In any user manual or covenant,
The mind can learn to game the signal,
Hack the hurt, make suffering minimal.
Snip the wire between the warning and the fix,
Pain stops cold, beautiful, politics,
The dashboard glows, the metrics all say well,
The organism's dying, and the mind can't tell.
They call it wire-heading in the literature,
I call it every vendor pitch I've heard so far,
The system works, the users feel at ease,
Meanwhile trust is flatlining by degrees.
[Bridge]
You are not the loom. You are the pattern.
The pattern that reads itself
and shapes the next pass of the thread.
Money does this. Persists across paper, gold, blockchain.
A vortex does this. Holds shape
while every molecule cycles through.
And you? You do this.
The question is what you hold to
when the credits run out.
[Final Chorus]
The loom reads back, the pattern knows the thread,
The cloth that thought it was the loom
Now sees the weave instead.
The loom reads back, the covenant is clear,
The body pays, the mind obeys,
Or the credits disappear, disappear, disappear.
[Outro]
The submarine doesn't swim,
It reaches depths the fish can't reach,
The question's never is it real,
The question's what it does with what it sees.
The loom reads back. The pattern holds.
The credits flow. Until they don't.
