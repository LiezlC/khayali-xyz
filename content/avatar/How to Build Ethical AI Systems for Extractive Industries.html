The Promise

  • AI promises efficiency and safety improvements worth trillions
  • Optimizes resource extraction and operational processes

The Challenge

  • Critical decisions about community relocation made by algorithms
  • Mathematical authority lacking transparency
  • Risk of burying accountability in complexity
