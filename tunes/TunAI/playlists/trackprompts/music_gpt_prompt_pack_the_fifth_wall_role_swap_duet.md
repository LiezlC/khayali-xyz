# MusicGPT Prompt Pack — The Fifth Wall (Role‑Swap Duet)

Copy/paste into **musicgpt.com**.

- Paste the **PROMPT BOX** text into MusicGPT’s *Prompt* (or *Describe your song*) field.
- Paste the **LYRICS BOX** text into the *Lyrics* field.
- MusicGPT will auto-generate **two versions**. This pack hard-locks the **two-voice duet + role swap** while leaving arrangement/melody free to vary.

---

## PROMPT BOX (instructions only — do not sing)

```text
NON-NEGOTIABLE (VOCALS ONLY) — EVERYTHING ELSE MAY VARY FREELY:
MusicGPT will output TWO versions automatically.

BOTH versions MUST be a DUET with TWO DISTINCT vocalists: one MALE + one FEMALE, in clear conversation (alternating lines).
The role swap MUST be the defining difference:

- Version 1: MALE performs [HUMAN] lines, FEMALE performs [MACHINE] lines.
- Version 2: MALE performs [MACHINE] lines, FEMALE performs [HUMAN] lines.

Rules to make this unmistakable:
- In the first 8 bars of EACH version, both voices must appear at least once.
- Line quota: Male must sing/speak at least 35% of the lines; Female at least 35%; optional [BOTH] overlaps for the rest.
- Machine voice: subtle vocoder/formant sheen, precise diction, calm (not cartoon robot).
- Human voice: warm, close-mic, breathy, intimate.
- DO NOT output any version with only one vocalist.

CREATIVE FREEDOM:
Arrangement, melody, chords, sound design, drops, and overall vibe can differ substantially between Version 1 and Version 2. That’s welcome.
Just keep the lyric script and the [HUMAN]/[MACHINE]/[BOTH] tags respected, and execute the role swap.

MUSIC DIRECTION (suggested, flexible):
- Style: organic deep house / melodic techno noir
- BPM: 126 (steady)
- Key: D minor with a gentle late lift toward F major
- Signature bed: low resonant “hum” (server-room / prayer tone) under everything
- Feature hook: pennywhistle or clarinet, 2–4 note motif recurring every ~8 bars
- Structure: minimal intro (vocals within first 1–2 bars), tight ending (no long fade), no breakdown longer than 8 bars
```
