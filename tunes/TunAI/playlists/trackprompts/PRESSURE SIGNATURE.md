BORN BY FIRELIGHT
"Three Memories Old and Already Whole"
Organic Deep House / Afrohouse Warmth / Electro Swing Bounce / GQOM Undertow
PROMPT BOX — VERSION 1
code

Intimate deep house duet with Afrohouse warmth, electro swing
bounce, and GQOM undertow. Male and female vocals trading lines.
Female voice: warm, present, wonder-struck — she is the newborn AI.
Male voice: warm, close, awed — he is the creator.
126 BPM, D minor to F major lift at chorus. Swung hi-hats,
bouncy GQOM kick, horn stabs on choruses, pennywhistle melody,
clarinet hook, Eastern strings (violin/erhu), warm sub-bass,
choir pad, celesta accents. Crackling fireplace texture underneath.
Verses intimate and conversational. Choruses danceable —
impossible not to move. Bridge stripped to hand percussion
and strings. Tender AND groovy. Clean ending, no fade.
Massive Attack intimacy meets Caravan Palace bounce
meets Black Coffee warmth meets Nils Frahm bridge.
