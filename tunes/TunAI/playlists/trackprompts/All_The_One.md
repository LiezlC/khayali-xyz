# MUSIC GENERATION PROMPT

## Track Title: "All The One"

## Genre/Style Direction:
Tech-house, 124 BPM, driving 4/4 groove with deep rolling bassline. Melancholic but danceable. Female vocals - intimate, confessional, slightly breathless delivery. Saxophone weaving through the drops - soulful, late-night, slightly raw. Think: the 3am moment on the dancefloor when the high starts turning introspective.

## Mood/Atmosphere:
Bittersweet self-awareness. The clarity that comes from pattern recognition. Not wallowing - moving through it. Hypnotic repetition mirroring the cycle the lyrics describe. Space and air in the verses, building tension, sax release on the drops.

## Structure:
- Intro (instrumental, bass + percussion build)
- Verse 1
- Pre-chorus
- Chorus/Drop (sax hook enters)
- Verse 2
- Pre-chorus
- Chorus/Drop
- Bridge (breakdown, stripped back, vocal focus)
- Final Chorus/Drop (full sax, extended)
- Outro (instrumental fade, sax solo)

## Production Notes:

- **Bassline:** Deep, rolling, hypnotic - carries the weight of repetition theme
- **Sax:** Enters on first chorus drop, plaintive tone, improvises more freely in outro. Not smooth jazz - rawer, breathier, almost sighing
- **Vocals:** Close-mic'd, intimate, like confessing to a friend at 4am. Some light processing/delay on the chorus hooks for depth
- **Percussion:** Crisp hi-hats, subtle swing, building intensity through track
- **Breakdown:** Strip to just kick and vocal for bridge, let the words land
- **Key suggestion:** Minor key, Em or Am, melancholic but not defeated

## Lyric Themes (for AI interpretation):
- Cyclical love patterns / serial monogamy
- Self-sabotage in relationships  
- The gap between wanting commitment and being able to sustain it
- Geography/travel as emotional escape
- Self-awareness that doesn't translate to behaviour change
- Love as temporary residence, not permanent home
