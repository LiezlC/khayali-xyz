TRACK PROMPT: “POINTERS IN THE DARK”
Genre + feel

Electro-swing noir duet with a trip-hop underlay.
Smoky club mood, vintage swing top layer, modern low-end pulse underneath. The contrast is the point.

Tempo + groove

104 BPM

Swing feel on the top percussion and brass stabs

Half-time trip-hop kick and sub underpinning everything

No EDM drop. Keep it taut.

Key + harmony

D minor

Jazz-inflected loop with minor 7 and minor 9 color

One brief lift to F major for 4 bars near the end, then return

Palette

Upright bass doubled with a soft sub

Brushed kit plus a dusty breakbeat layer

Rhodes or dark piano comping

Clarinet as the “continuous” voice motif

Muted trumpet stabs as “discrete” punctuation

Very subtle room hum, almost subliminal

Vocal roles

Machine voice: clipped, rhythmic, spoken-sung. Quantized. Short phrases that land like operators.

Human voice: melodic, sustained, flexible timing. Longer vowels, legato. More air.

Structure (3:30–4:10)

Intro (machine sets primitives) → Verse 1 → Chorus → Verse 2 → Chorus → Bridge (translation problem) → Final chorus → Outro (two motifs braid, then cut)

Lyrics (strictly on the Bach themes)

Model running.
Signal in.
Pointer set.

VERSE 1 (Human, sung, legato)
I don’t meet the world in pieces,
I meet it as a shape in motion,
a field of almost-knowing,
edges, distance, weight.
Before I name a thing,
I feel where it sits.

High bandwidth.

the whole before the parts.
Then language clicks its little hooks
into the moving dark.
Give me a name, I’ll point to it,
give me a rule, I’ll try.
We live between the shape of it
and the reason why.
