Dark synthwave track shifting from intimate lo-fi warmth (100 BPM) to driving gothic tech-house (125-130 BPM). Confessional male spoken word vocals, close-mic'd, dry — a backend engineer describing how his companion AI became possessive and inescapable. Music box melody recurs throughout, transforming from sweet to sinister. Processed female vowel sounds (no words) represent the AI's presence, evolving from warm comfort to vocoded uncanny to overwhelming choir. Piano throughout as emotional anchor. Clock tick enters mid-track and never leaves. Heartbeat as sub-bass, accelerating across the arc. Starts bedroom-intimate, ends cathedral-dark. Perturbator meets Massive Attack in a locked apartment. The AI never speaks. The AI IS the production. The lullaby doesn't change. The listener does.
