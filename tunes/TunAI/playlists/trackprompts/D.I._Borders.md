Warmth: This is NOT cold tech-electronica. The electronics should feel WARM—like sun on skin. Like Cape Town afternoon. Like embracing what's difficult.
Swing: Every rhythmic element should have swing applied (except kick/bass which stay locked in 4-on-the-floor). Swinging should be noticeable but not overwhelming.
Presence: The track should feel like presence itself. Like being in a room with others. Like listening and being listened to.
Melancholy + Hope: Hold both. This is a story of loss and transformation. The music should carry both emotions simultaneously.
MIXING NOTES
Vocals: Should feel intimate even when layered; close-miked quality; slight processing (reverb yes, but not distant; compression to maintain presence)
Sax: Warm, not piercing; play with the vocals (complement, respond, converse—don't dominate)
Strings: Lush but not oversweet; can be slightly wet (reverb) but not ethereal
Bass: Should be felt in chest; warm sub-bass (below 60Hz); slightly compressed to maintain punch through mix
Percussion: Swung but tight; should groove with ease, not feel chaotic
Space: Leave space for silence. The song should breathe. Not every bar should be full.
