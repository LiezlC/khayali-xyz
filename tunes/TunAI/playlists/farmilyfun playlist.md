Production notes

Clarinet riff should be iconic and repeat between vocal lines.

Muted trumpet stabs should answer the vocal (short call/response).

Keep the groove tight, no long instrumental solos.

If you do a break, max 8 bars, then vocals return.

End with a crisp “big band stop” style hit, not a fade.
